Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Charlaa ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Charlaa mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Phone number used for authentication.
  • Profile information: Name, age, gender, photos, religious values, family values, education, profession, and other details you provide.
  • Location data: Approximate location to show relevant matches nearby (only when you grant permission).
  • Usage data: App interactions, likes, matches, and messages within the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with compatible users based on your preferences and values.
  • To enable communication between matched users.
  • To improve app features and user experience.
  • To moderate content and maintain community safety.
  • To send important app-related notifications.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party services (e.g., Supabase for database, Expo for push notifications) that help us operate the app under strict confidentiality.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request account deletion at any time through the app settings. Upon deletion, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure database storage to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Charlaa is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of Charlaa after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
