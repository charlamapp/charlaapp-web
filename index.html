Our Stance

We Built Charlaa Because
Dating Apps Got It Wrong

Most apps are built around swiping, flirting, and casual connections. We believe that is disrespectful to people who hold their values close. Charlaa was built as an alternative — for those who see marriage as a sacred commitment, not a game.

🚫

No Flirting Culture

Charlaa has zero tolerance for casual, disrespectful, or flirtatious behavior. Every interaction is expected to be purposeful and respectful.

🕌

Rooted in Religious Values

Whether you are Muslim or belong to another faith tradition, Charlaa respects and centers religious values as the foundation of a strong marriage.

🌍

Honoring Cultural Identity

We believe cultural roots and traditions are a strength. Charlaa helps you find someone who shares and respects your background.

💍

Marriage is the Goal

Every profile on Charlaa is created with one clear intention — finding a life partner for a serious, committed marriage.
